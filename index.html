Published work

IET Publishing · Forthcoming book chapter · July 2026

A Calibrated Multimodal AI System for Real-Time, Treatment-Specific Mortality Prediction in the ICU

Designed a multimodal model combining ResNet-50 and ClinicalBERT, trained on 612K+ radiographs from 42K+ ICU patients. The system outputs treatment-specific counterfactual predictions, letting clinicians reason in real time about how a patient's risk evolves under alternative fluid, oxygen, and vasopressor strategies.

A prospective stepped-wedge cluster-randomized evaluation across 2,000 ICU patients showed potential for improving early risk detection and supporting timely treatment modifications.
